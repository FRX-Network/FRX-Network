Information about cookies

A cookie is a small file that is stored on your computer and which contains information about your visit to our site. When you click on a new link, the information is sent back to us. Cookies are used to improve the website for you.
We do not store any personal information via cookies and information can never be traced to you.
We use both temporary cookies that are deleted immediately when you leave our site and permanent cookies that are used to see how many repeat visitors the website has.

"I DO NOT ACCEPT COOKIES"

If you do not accept cookies, you can make settings in your browser. For example, you can set that you automatically refuse the storage of cookies or that you will receive information when a website requests to store a cookie. You can also clear stored cookies.
