About FRX

FRX was founded by @lollibtw to provide the best FREE practice for the fortnite community. FRX is a fast growing, active and a welcoming server were anyone can join, no matter where you're from! The server is being worked on and improved every single day to make sure it's as good as it can get! 

What we offer:
+ Daily Money ZoneWars (Read more here)
+ Channels for all regions to find people to play with
+ Active and fast growing community  
+ Self promotion channel to help you grow your socials! 
And A LOT more!
